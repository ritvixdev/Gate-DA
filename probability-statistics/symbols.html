Symbols Glossary

Every notation used across Probability & Statistics — how to say it, what it means, and a quick example.

1 Probability
SymbolRead asMeaning
$S$, $\Omega$"sample space"all possible outcomes
$P(A)$"P of A"probability of event $A$ (in $[0,1]$)
$A^c$"A complement"$A$ does not happen; $P(A^c)=1-P(A)$
$A\cup B$ / $A\cap B$"union / intersection"or / and
$P(A\mid B)$"A given B"conditional probability
$\varnothing$"empty set"impossible event
2 Random variables & distributions
SymbolRead asMeaning
$X,\ Y$"X, Y"random variables
$E[X]$, $\mu$"expectation / mean"average value
$\operatorname{Var}(X)$, $\sigma^2$"variance"spread (squared); $\sigma$ = std dev
$p(x)$ / $f(x)$"PMF / PDF"$P(X=x)$ / density
$F(x)$"CDF"$P(X\le x)$
$\binom{n}{k}$"n choose k"combinations
$\lambda$"lambda"rate (Poisson / Exponential)
3 Joint & relationships
SymbolRead asMeaning
$p(x,y)$, $f(x,y)$"joint"joint distribution of two variables
$\operatorname{Cov}(X,Y)$"covariance"$E[XY]-E[X]E[Y]$
$\rho$"rho"correlation, in $[-1,1]$
4 Statistics & hypothesis testing
SymbolRead asMeaning
$\bar{x}$"x-bar"sample mean
$s^2$, $s$"sample variance / std"spread (divide by $n-1$)
$Q_1,Q_2,Q_3$, IQR"quartiles, IQR"25/50/75th percentiles; $Q_3-Q_1$
$H_0,\ H_1$"null, alternative"the hypotheses being tested
$\alpha$"alpha"significance level (false-alarm rate)
$z,\ t$"z, t"test statistics (mean tests)
$\chi^2$, $F$"chi-square, F"count test / variance-ratio test
