Optimization
for GATE DA

Every model "learns" by minimizing something. This subject is how — finding the lowest point of a function, by calculus and by gradient descent, with a lab where you watch it roll downhill.

🎯 Why it matters

Optimization is the engine of machine learning: training = minimizing a loss. GATE DA tests the core ideas — finding minima with derivatives, the role of the gradient and Hessian, and how gradient descent steps toward the answer.
