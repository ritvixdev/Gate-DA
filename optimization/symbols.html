Symbols Glossary

Every notation used across Optimization — how to say it, what it means, and a quick example.

1 Objective & optima
SymbolRead asMeaning
$f(x)$"f of x"objective / cost function
$x^*$"x star"the optimal point (minimizer)
$\min f$ / $\max f$"min / max"smallest / largest value
$\arg\min f$"argmin"the $x$ that achieves the min
s.t."subject to"introduces constraints
2 Derivatives & curvature
SymbolRead asMeaning
$f'(x)$, $\tfrac{df}{dx}$"f prime"first derivative (slope)
$f''(x)$"f double prime"second derivative (curvature)
$\nabla f$"grad f"gradient = vector of first partials
$\partial f/\partial x_i$"partial"slope in the $x_i$ direction
$H$, $\nabla^2 f$"Hessian"matrix of second partials
3 Gradient descent & conditions
SymbolRead asMeaning
$x_k$"x sub k"point at iteration $k$
$\eta$"eta"learning rate (step size)
$-\nabla f$"minus grad f"steepest-descent direction
PD / ND"positive/negative definite"all eigenvalues $\gt0$ / $\lt0$ → min / max
saddle"saddle point"$\nabla f=0$, Hessian indefinite
