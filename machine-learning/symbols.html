Symbols Glossary

Every notation used across Basic Machine Learning — how to say it, what it means, and a quick example.

1 Regression & least-squares
SymbolRead asMeaning
$y$ / $\hat{y}$"y / y-hat"actual / predicted target
$x,\ x_j$"x, x j"feature(s) / inputs
$\beta_0,\ \beta_j$"betas"intercept / coefficients
$e_i$"residual"$y_i-\hat{y}_i$
SSE / RSS"squared error"$\sum e_i^2$ (least-squares minimizes it)
$X^{\mathsf T}X\beta=X^{\mathsf T}y$"normal equations"$\beta=(X^{\mathsf T}X)^{-1}X^{\mathsf T}y$
$R^2$"R squared"fraction of variance explained
2 Classification (logistic, kNN)
SymbolRead asMeaning
$\sigma(z)$"sigmoid"$1/(1+e^{-z})$, in $(0,1)$
$z$"score"linear score $\beta_0+\beta^{\mathsf T}x$
$\hat{p}$"p-hat"predicted probability of class 1
$k$ (kNN)"k"number of neighbors that vote
$d(a,b)$"distance"Euclidean $\sqrt{\sum(a_i-b_i)^2}$
3 Clustering & validation
SymbolRead asMeaning
$k$ (k-means)"k"number of clusters
$\mu_j$"mu j"centroid (cluster mean)
WCSS / inertia"inertia"within-cluster sum of squares
$k$ (folds)"k-fold"number of CV folds
LOOCV"leave-one-out"$k=n$
